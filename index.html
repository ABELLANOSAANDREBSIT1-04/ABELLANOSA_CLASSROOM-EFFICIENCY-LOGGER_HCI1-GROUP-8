Support

Having trouble using the Classroom Efficiency Logger? Check the common issues below or contact our IT team directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • My submission didn't go through. — Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem persists, contact the IT Office.
  • I entered the wrong subject code. — Please reach out to your instructor or the Registrar's Office to have the entry corrected.
  • The form keeps resetting. — Make sure you are using a supported browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox). Avoid using private/incognito mode.
  • I can't find my section code. — Your section code is printed on your Certificate of Registration (COR). Contact your adviser if you need help.
  • Is my feedback anonymous? — Individual responses are handled confidentially by the Academic Affairs office and are used only for improving class delivery.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a feedback form, we collect the following information:

  • Subject or activity code
  • Section identifier
  • Rating (1–5 scale)
  • Optional comments or complaints

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your full name, student ID, or email address unless you voluntarily provide it in the comments field.

2. How We Use Your Information

Submitted data is used solely to evaluate and improve the quality of classroom activities and teaching delivery at PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College. Data may be aggregated for academic reporting.

3. Data Storage & Security

All submissions are stored on secured college servers and are accessible only to authorized academic and administrative personnel. We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

4. Data Retention

Feedback records are retained for a maximum of three (3) academic years, after which they are anonymized or securely deleted.

5. Your Rights

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173), you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data you have submitted. Contact our Data Privacy Officer at privacy@phinmacdo.edu.ph.

6. Contact

For privacy-related concerns, please email privacy@phinmacdo.edu.ph.

About Us

PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College

PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College (PHINMA CDO) is a private higher education institution committed to delivering quality, affordable education to students in Northern Mindanao and beyond. As part of the PHINMA Education Network, the college upholds a culture of excellence, integrity, and service.

About the Classroom Efficiency Logger

The Classroom Efficiency Logger is a digital feedback tool developed under the Student Information System (SIS) initiative. It allows students to submit structured evaluations of classroom activities, helping faculty and administration continuously improve the quality of learning experiences.

Our Goals

  • Empower students to voice their learning experience
  • Provide faculty with timely, actionable feedback
  • Support data-driven academic improvement decisions
  • Promote transparency and accountability in classroom delivery
